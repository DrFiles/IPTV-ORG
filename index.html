Indian IPTV App Overview

The Indian IPTV App is a comprehensive platform that allows users to stream over 500 Indian TV channels directly from their devices. The app provides a seamless streaming experience with a wide variety of channels, catering to diverse preferences and needs.

Key Features:

  • 500+ Channels: Access to a vast selection of Indian TV channels, including news, entertainment, sports, movies, and regional content.
  • Seamless Streaming: Enjoy uninterrupted streaming with high-quality video playback and minimal buffering.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate the app with ease, thanks to its intuitive design and responsive controls.
  • Multi-Device Support: Stream your favorite content on multiple devices, whether it's a smartphone, tablet, or smart TV.

Whether you're looking to catch up on the latest news, watch your favorite TV shows, or enjoy live sports, the Indian IPTV App has something for everyone. Explore a world of entertainment at your fingertips!

1-Day API Update

Date: 8/14/2024

Objective

To implement minor updates to the Indian IPTV App API for enhanced performance and bug fixes. This update will focus on ensuring that the streaming experience remains seamless and that the API is aligned with the latest app functionalities.

Details of the Update

  • Bug Fixes:
    • Resolved an issue where certain regional channels were intermittently failing to load.
    • Fixed a bug that caused the app to crash when users attempted to access the channel list in offline mode.
    • Corrected an error in the API that resulted in incorrect EPG (Electronic Program Guide) data for certain channels.
  • Performance Enhancements:
    • Optimized the API's data retrieval process to reduce latency and improve channel loading times.
    • Implemented caching improvements to decrease server load and enhance streaming stability.
  • New Features:
    • Added support for additional metadata in the API response, allowing for richer content descriptions and improved user experience.
    • Enabled dynamic updating of channel lineups without requiring app updates, ensuring users have access to the latest available channels.

Impact on Users

Users may experience brief downtime (up to 15 minutes) during the update window. After the update, users should notice faster channel loading times and fewer issues with channel availability.

Testing and Validation

Comprehensive testing will be conducted before and after the update to ensure all changes have been implemented successfully. Monitoring tools will be used to track the API's performance post-update and to identify any issues that may arise.
